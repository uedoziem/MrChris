What exactly is Dropshipping?


Dropshipping is a branch of Ecommerce, with a business model that allows entrepreneurs to sell products that are manufactured, warehoused and shipped by vendors from their own online store.


What this means is this:


  • You do not manufacture the products
  • You do not warehouse the products
  • You do not ship/deliver the products


And surprisingly, you may not even see the product at all.


Dropshipping is a great option for first-time entrepreneurs. It frees up time that can be refocused on marketing and provides a low-risk way for small businesses to test out new product ideas without having to own and pay for the product upfront.

DROPSHIPPING BUSINESS MODEL

In the pictorial description above,

  • A dropshipper owns an online store called "Your Store" that sells clothes. He then, for instance, added a markup price of $30 to each of his products on his store.
  • A Customer goes to his store and orders and purchases a cloth at $100.
  • Because your store is already connected to a platform, your vendor is automatically notified and delivers the goods to your customer
  • Then the profit of $30 becomes yours and is credited to your account

Now, i want you to notice something exciting about this business model:

  • The dropshipper did not see the product!
  • The dropshipper did not manufacture the product!
  • The dropshipper did not deliver the product!


All was done automatically from the dropshipper's online store that is connected to a platform. All the dropshipper must do are:


  • Enure that he/she is driving traffic to his/her online store
  • Enure that the traffic he/she brings purchases the goods in his/her online store
